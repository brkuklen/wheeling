The Wheel Strategy

A mechanical guide and an interactive training quest for the cash-secured-put & covered-call options strategy.

The wheel is a defined, repeatable options-income strategy: sell cash-secured puts on stocks you'd be happy to own; if assigned, sell covered calls against the shares until called away; then start the cycle again.

These two pages are companion materials — read the guide first, then walk the quest to internalize the mechanics.
